HISTORY OF COUNTER-STRIKE

Counter-Strike is a series of multiplayer first-person shooter video games in which players assume the roles of terrorists and counter-terrorists.
It has evolved over the years, starting from a Half-Life modification to becoming one of the most iconic esports titles in gaming history.
