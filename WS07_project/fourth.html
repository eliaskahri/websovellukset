THE BASICS OF THE GAME AND OTHER FEATURES

The most popular mode among the other modes is bomb defusal. Terrorists must plant the bomb at
one of the two bombsites, bombsite A or bombsite B. On otherside there are the counter-terrorists who
must stop them or defuse the bomb if it's planted. Counter-Strikes most important thing is communication.
Without communication you most likely aren't victorious. The players need to communicate in voice-chat
about strategies, share information about the enemy and support each other with utility, like grenades,
smoke grenades and flashbanges.
